How to avoid contact with ghosts


there are some place in a house you should try to avoid

  • mirrors (especially at night!)
  • dark cramped bathrooms (especially when you hear water dripping!)
  • attics and basements (especially when there are stories about how they are haunted!)
  • under the bed (especially when you hear breathing from underneath!)
but this probably looks too familiar if you just read how to get in contact with ghosts, so here are some other ideas!
  • salt and glutinous rice (they are tasty and powerful)
  • people (because how can one ghost fight a crowd of humans!)
  • wait until sunrise (hang on just a little bit longer!)
  • pretend you are one of them (they are not as smart as you think...?)
